Our roles
Youth-workers, Project Managers, Parkour Instructors,Aerial instructors, Circus Skill teachers, Young Leaders, Trustees, Young Trustees, Development Officers, SEN support workers.

Volunteers

We wouldn't be able to do what we do without our amazing and committed volunteers, both young and old. Our volunteers are able to access free DBS checks, and professional development opportunities such as Health and Safety, First Aid and Safeguarding Training, and much more.

Specialist skill teachers

Our specialist skill instructors teach a range of skills, such as Aerial Silks, Aerial Hoop, Acrobatics, Acrobalance, Parkour, Free Running, Juggling, Poi/Club/Staff twirling, Unicycling, and much more.

Young trainees

Our young leadership program comprises of 4 stages, Our Young Leadership program aims to empower young people aged 15-25 by providing them with valuable leadership training and opportunities within the circus community. We have three levels of participation: trainee, assistant leader, and leader. Trainees receive foundational training in essential leadership skills such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving, access to in-house training and e-learning content. As they progress to the assistant leader level, they gain hands- on experience in mentoring and guiding others, as well as in- person training courses such as First Aid. Finally, as leaders, participants have the chance to lead their own projects and inspire others. Our program is designed to foster personal growth, develop leadership abilities, and make a positive impact.

Qualified youth workers

Our qualified youth workers work directly with children and young people, helping them to build life skills and confidence, develop healthy relationships and make decisions that are right for them.
